Our Activities

Discover the various ways we support and empower our community of children with rare diseases and their families.

Making a Difference

At KIDS Rare Hellas, we believe in a holistic approach to supporting children with rare diseases and their families. Our activities span across education, advocacy, community support, and research collaboration.

Our Core Activities

Educational Programs

We offer a range of educational programs designed to inform and empower patients, families, and healthcare professionals about rare diseases.

  • Workshops for patients and families
  • Seminars for healthcare professionals
  • School awareness programs

Advocacy Initiatives

We work tirelessly to advocate for the rights and needs of children with rare diseases at local and national levels.

  • Policy recommendations
  • Collaboration with policymakers
  • Public awareness campaigns

Support Services

We provide various support services to help families navigate the challenges of living with a rare disease.

  • Support groups and counseling
  • Resource navigation assistance
  • Respite care programs

Research Collaboration

We collaborate with researchers and medical professionals to advance understanding and treatment of rare diseases.

  • Research partnerships
  • Patient registry development
  • Clinical trial information sharing
