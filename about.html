About KIDS Rare Hellas

Empowering children with rare diseases and their families in Greece since 2024.

Our Story

KIDS Rare Hellas was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate individuals who recognized the unique challenges faced by children with rare diseases in Greece. Our founders, including medical professionals, parents of affected children, and advocates, came together with a shared vision: to create a supportive community and improve the lives of these special children.

Since our inception, we've grown from a small, local initiative to a nationwide organization, touching the lives of hundreds of families across Greece. Our journey has been one of continuous learning, growth, and unwavering commitment to our mission.

Our Mission

Kids Rare Hellas is dedicated to empowering young patients with rare diseases in Greece. We strive to create a supportive community, raise awareness, and improve healthcare outcomes for these brave individuals.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child with a rare disease has access to the best possible care, support, and opportunities to thrive. We work tirelessly to make this vision a reality in Greece.

Our Approach

Community Building

We create spaces for families to connect, share experiences, and support each other.

Advocacy

We advocate for better policies, increased funding, and improved healthcare for rare diseases.

Education

We provide educational resources and programs for families, healthcare providers, and the public.

Support Services

We offer various support services to help families navigate the challenges of rare diseases.
